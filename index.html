Objective of Hexapod Spider Robot

The evaluation of the study will be primarily conducted in a small-scale simulations or controlled environment.

Control

To provide the operator with a remote control and monitoring system for the hexapod spider robot.

Versatility

To assess the agility, stability and adaptability of the hexapod spider robot while navigating in difficult terrain, such as confined areas and uneven terrain.
